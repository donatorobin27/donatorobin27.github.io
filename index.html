Our Story

Bridging the Ancient
& the Present

At DS T Services, we believe that the past holds the keys to extraordinary value — culturally, historically, and financially. Founded on a deep passion for human heritage, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary powerhouse operating at the intersection of archaeological science, rare antiquities trading, and distinguished real estate.

Our team of seasoned archaeologists, certified antique appraisers, and real estate specialists work in seamless harmony to deliver unmatched expertise across every engagement. From excavating ancient ruins to placing irreplaceable artifacts in the hands of worthy collectors — and identifying heritage properties of enduring worth — we are dedicated to preserving legacy while creating opportunity.

With operations spanning continents and partnerships with leading museums, collectors, and property investors, DS T Services stands as a trusted guardian of history and a bold architect of the future.

Latest Discoveries

Featured Discovery

Unveiling the Lost Mosaic of the Eastern Meridian

Deep within a remote hillside excavation, our team uncovered a breathtaking 2nd-century mosaic spanning over 14 square metres — its vivid geometric patterns and mythological scenes remarkably intact. Preliminary dating places its origins in the late Roman provincial era, making it one of the most significant finds of the decade. The mosaic is currently undergoing meticulous restoration in our conservation lab, with plans for museum display and academic publication.
